    Sample preparation

    • Sample vial: reusable 1 ml borosilicate glass vial (WHEATON W986284NG) + black cap + blue septa (Analytical Columns C234115)
    • Sample size: 100–150 µg
    • Sequence (standards 12 + samples 30): Carrara Z × 3, Atlantis II, 10 samples, Carrara Z × 2, 10 samples, Carrara Z × 2, 10 samples, Atlantis II, Carrara Z × 3

    Procedures

    Monday

    1. Prep–Water Trap Chiller On
    2. AC, ML, MH
    3. Test acid drop
      1. Put tissue under needle
      2. Prep–Dispense Acid
      3. Check acid drop after ~5 mins. If not, repeat the last step.
      4. Redo 2 & 3
    4. 45–60 mins

    Monday to Thursday

    1. Check status: Finished Autorun
    2. Remove LN2 tubes from dewar
    3. Put lids on samples
    4. Flush sample needle with He gas
      1. Prep–Move Gilson To Next Vial. If Gilson does not move: Prep–Initialise Gilson, then try again.
      2. AB, MH
      3. Check: AD
      4. AE
      5. Connect He gas, open cylinder valve, open regulator valve
      6. Wipe off acid
      7. Check flow rate through the needle
      8. Disonnect He gas
    5. Pump out
      1. Put on an old vial on needle: holes inside the vial & DO NOT touch the acid
      2. ML, AD, until ML vac ≈ 1.0e-3 (take out old sample vials in the heater block)
      3. Prep–Initialise Gilson
    6. Remove old vial
    7. AB, ML
    8. MH (auto ML)
    9. Refill reference gas
      1. Set reference gas bellow to 3100 (Stage 2)
      2. LV, RP, RV, until LV vac ≈ 1.0e-3 (Put new sample vials into the heater block)
      3. Inlet–Toggle C/O Valve, until 44 < 4e-11
      4. RM
      5. Check SP
      6. RI
      7. Manually operate 2 toggle valves (upper, lower) on reference gas cylinder
        1. L: pump out reference gas between the two valves 30s
        2. L, U: release reference gas into the space between L and U 30s
        3. LV
        4. RP (Stage 2)
        5. U, L: refill reference gas 30s (edit batch MPRPCM)
        6. L
      8. Measure reference gas
        1. RI, RP
        2. Set reference gas bellow to 0, until bellow fully open
        3. RM
        4. Check 44 ≈ 2.5e-9
    10. Test–Peak Center
    11. Close scan
    12. Update sample table in batch MPRPCM
      1. Analysis–Autorun Batch Edit–select MPRPCM from the list
      2. MPRPCM: Setup
      3. Release all
      4. Enter new sample numbers, then OK–OK
    13. Reboot by Ctrl+Alt+Del
    14. Fill LN2 dewar
      • wear PPE
      • efficient transfer is achieved at 5–10 psi
      • filled to 90% of their net capacity to reduce the risk of spillage
      • 20L, record on the notebook
    15. Check two tubes: ~2 cm from the end of the wood stick, then put them into the LN2 dewar
    16. Check connecting pipe
    17. Check paper
    18. Press LF/FF button twice? to feed paper forward until 3 holes are out
    19. Analysis–Autorun Start/Stop–select MPRPCM from the list, Run

    Friday Standby

    1. Check status: Finished Autorun
    2. Remove LN2 tubes from dewar
    3. Prep–Water Trap Chiller Off
    4. ML, AB (check: AC)
    5. LV, RP, RF, RV, RM, until LV vac ≈ 1.0e-3
    6. HV

    CO2 from organic matter

    1. HV
    2. RP, RF, check RI
    3. Disconnect Carrara Z reference gas cylinder
    4. Connect organic reference gas cylinder
    5. Fill organic reference gas
      1. Set reference gas bellow to 1000
      2. ? LV, RP, RF, RV, RM, until LV vac ≈ 1.0e-3
      3. Inlet–Toggle C/O Valve, until 44 < 4e-11
      4. RM
      5. ? Check SP
      6. RI
      7. Manually operate 2 toggle valves (upper, lower) on organic reference gas cylinder
        1. L: pump out reference gas between the two valves 30s
        2. L, U: release reference gas into the space between L and U 30s
        3. LV
        4. RP
        5. U, L: refill reference gas 30s
        6. L
      8. Measure organic reference gas
        1. RI, RP, RF
        2. RM
        3. Tune reference gas bellow (squeeze, larger value) ⟹ 44 ≈ 6e-9
        4. ? RV
        5. ? disable changeover
        6. Manually analyse against reference gas itself (DACP–CO2, Sample name: ORG GAS REF). Disconnect organic reference gas cylinder
    6. ? Enable changeover
    7. (Check RI) Cut glass sample tube, insert into metal tube (from the simple end), connect (the more complicated end with a filter) to reference gas inlet
    8. Fill sample gas
      1. Set sample gas bellow to 0
      2. ? LV, RI, RP, SP, until LV vac ≈ 1.0e-3
      3. SF, SV, SM, until LV vac ≈ 1.0e-3
      4. Inlet–Toggle C/O Valve, until 44 < 4e-11
      5. LV
      6. Break sample tube, until 44 is stable
    9. Measure sample gas
      1. RI, SF (store sample gas in between)
      2. Tune sample gas bellow (squeeze, larger value) ⟹ 44 ≈ 6e-9, record the value
      3. Inlet–Toggle C/O Valve, tune reference gas bellow (squeeze, larger value) ⟹ sample − reference ≈ 0.08
      4. Inlet–Toggle C/O Valve, tune sample gas bellow (squeeze, larger value) ⟹ sample − reference ≈ 0.08
      5. RV, SV
      6. Manually analyse (DACP–CO2, Sample name: …). Disconnect sample gas tube
    10. Repeat 6–8. Can pump sample gas in between RI and SF (LV) when the analysis for current sample gas appears to be OK
    11. (Check RI) Connect Carrara Z reference gas cylinder
    12. Standby
      1. Changeover → waste
      2. ? Set both bellows to 0
      3. Pump all that is left of SI: LV, open RI…, until LV vac ≈ 1.0e-3
      4. RI
      5. HV

    Misc.

    • Cold fingers: click stop before clicking heat/cool
    • Scan config for OCR : 600 dpi, Gray scale, TIFF
    • Replenish reference gas:
      • Stage 1: RP, gradually decrease reference gas bellow (increase bellow size) through time
        • until 2024-01-23: 1400
      • Stage 2: RP, gradually decrease reference gas bellow (increase bellow size) through time
        • after 2024-01-23: 3100
    • Valve controller:
      • 3 seals

    Data quality control

    • Ion Gauge for Sample and Reference are similar, difference < 0.2e-8
    • Error 1051 is OK

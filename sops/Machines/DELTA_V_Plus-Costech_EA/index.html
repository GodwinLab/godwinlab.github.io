    Change chemicals

    per >1000 samples, can be ~1300

    1. Prepare new
      • Cr tube (Elemental Microanalysis X1220) & Cu tube (Elemental Microanalysis X1222)
      • 2 black rubber O-rings (Elemental Microanalysis E1041)
      • 2 silicone/PTFE seals (Elemental Microanalysis E1021)
    2. Prepare new Cr tube & Cu tube
      1. Deash metal insert, put into Cr tube
      2. For better seal: take out extra quartz wool (Elemtex LECOÂ® 502-177), push back so that no fibers stick out
      3. Put a black rubber O-ring on the top end, 3? mm from the top (ref: packing diagrams in the manual)
    3. Take out 2 tubes (Cr tube, and then Cu tube)
      1. Turn off source
      2. Turn off needle valve (Check: Vac xe-6 â†’ xe-8)
      3. Press F3: gas flow off
      4. Wear Kevlar gloves (prevent cut and heat)
      5. Disconnect bottom
        1. open bigger closure on the left of digital display
        2. move aside 2 metal holder (different height, so do not mix-up)
        3. unscrew alternately while holding metal tube
        4. put aside metal tube (do not mix-up)
        5. take off metal tube locking nut together with a metal O-ring and a black rubber O-ring
      6. Disconnect top
      7. Take out very hot tube (away from PFA tube)
    4. Install 2 tubes (left Cr tube, and then right Cu tube)
      1. Insert tube, connect top
      2. Connect bottom
      3. Press F3: gas flow on, leak check: all 4 connections, Press F3: gas flow off
    5. Change water trap Mg(ClO4)2 tube
      • Open smaller closure beneath the digital display
      • Unscrew both ends, take off, clear
      • Insert quartz wool on one end, push it to the other end with an Allen key
      • Refill Mg(ClO4)2 (Acros Organics 413461000) with a funnel. Shake, but not too tight.
      • Insert quartz wool. Make sure no fibers stick out on both ends.
      • Install Mg(ClO4)2 tube with PTFE side of both seals facing upwards
      • Press F3: gas flow on, leak check (use paper to protect wires), Press F3: gas flow off
    6. Rest count: Init
    7. ConFlo IV: Sample dilution max (98%)
    8. Turn on needle valve (Check: Vac xe-8 â†’ xe-6)
    9. Turn on source
    10. Check flow rate
      1. front panel He: 80 mL/min (idle: ~10)
      2. front panel O2: 20 mL/min (idle: 0)
      3. autosampler He: 80–100 mL/min (idle: 0)

    Replace He

    Same as DELTA_V, but switch the cylinders as quickly as possible to prevent chemicals (esp. Cu) from oxidising due to introduction of air.

    Determine dilution

    Do this after changing filament

    1. Check previous sensitivity: ConFlo IV Interface Show Reference Gas Dilution
    2. Ref Dil 0, peak center, Auto focus for CO2
    3. Do the same for N2
    4. Determine Reference Gas Dilution, ~10 min (auto test combinations: shown in ConFlo IV Diagnosis)
    5. Run OnOff

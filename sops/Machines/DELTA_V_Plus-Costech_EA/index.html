    Procedures

    Work–Enter

    Standby–Enter (gas flow off)

    Deash

    200 samples

    1. Turn off source
    2. Turn off needle valve (Check: Vac 1e-6 → 1e-7)
    3. Press F3: gas flow off
    4. Deash
      1. Take out metal insert and deash
      2. Check depth and refill quartz wool
      3. Put back metal insert
    5. Press F3: gas flow on, leak check
    6. Press F3: gas flow off
    7. ConFlo IV: Sample dilution max (98%)
    8. Turn on needle valve (Check: Vac 1e-7 → 1e-6)
    9. Turn on source

    Change chemicals

    >1000 samples, can be ~1300

    1. Take out 2 tubes
      1. Turn off source
      2. Turn off needle valve (Check: Vac 1e-6 → 1e-7)
      3. Press F3: gas flow off
      4. Wear Kevlar gloves
      5. Disconnect top of each tube
      6. Disconnect bottom of each tube
      7. Take out
    2. Prepare news tubes:
      1. Deash metal insert, put into Cr tube
      2. Take out extra quartz wool for better seal
      3. Put on rubber o-ring
    3. Install 2 tubes
      1. Connect top of Cr tube
      2. Connect bottom of each tube
        • metal o-ring–rubber o-ring
      3. Connect top of Cu tube
      4. Press F3: gas flow on, leak check: all 4 connections, Press F3: gas flow off
    4. Change MgCl2 tube for water trap (2 seals: PTFE side facing upwards)
      • Shake, not too tight
      • Press F3: gas flow on, leak check (use paper to protect wires), Press F3: gas flow off
    5. Rest count: Init
    6. ConFlo IV: Sample dilution max (98%)
    7. Turn on needle valve (Check: Vac 1e-7 → 1e-6)
    8. Turn on source

    Determine dilution

    Do this after changing filament

    1. Check previous sensitivity: ConFlo IV Interface Show Reference Gas Dilution
    2. Ref Dil 0, peak center, Auto focus for CO2
    3. Do the same for N2
    4. Determine Reference Gas Dilution, ~10 min (auto test combinations: shown in ConFlo IV Diagnosis)
    5. Run OnOff

    2. Turn on

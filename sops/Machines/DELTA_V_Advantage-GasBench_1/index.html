    Sample preparation

    • Sample vial: disposable 12 ml borosilicate glass vial (Labco 9RK8)
    • Sample size: 100–200 µg
    • ? Sequence (24 dummies + standards 16 + samples 50): 8 bypass (old Carrara Z), Carrara Z × 3, Fletton clay, 8 bypass (old samples/standards), 10 samples, Carrara Z × 2, 10 samples, Carrara Z × 2, 10 samples, Carrara Z × 2, 10 samples, Carrara Z × 2, 10 samples, Fletton clay, Carrara Z × 3, 8 acid dump

    Procedures

    1. Check acid pump
      1. Forward
      2. manually click button on acid pump multiple times: should be <4s per drop
      3. wipe off acid
    2. Refill acid (stock is on SIRA)
    3. Put on dummies/standards/samples
    4. Needle maintenance
      • curve

    8. Check first bypass: peaks should decrease

      1. Check out flow from the glass capillary

    • Air in pump:
      1. disconnect the metal tube and manually click button on acid pump
      2. if too much air, pump DI water until it starts to spout, and then pump acid (discard acid after pumping)

    Picarro L2140-i

    Sample preparation

    1. 1.5 mL sample (nominal ⌀ 9 mm; vial: 11.6×32 mm Fisherbrand 10162512; cap: silicone/PTFE septa, thickness 1 mm, Fisherbrand 11787567)
    2. Sequence: DI water, DY-2 × 2, BOTTY × 2, SPIT 2 × 2, (5 samples, BOTTY), …
    3. 200 µl standard in reusable micro-insert (MACHEREY‑NAGEL 702818 0.1 mL), mark on caps
      • DY-2: δ18O = −23.08‰
      • BOTTY: δ18O = −7.4‰
      • SPIT-2: δ18O = −0.15‰

    Tips:

    • do not tighten caps too much

    • no bubbles

      • release thumb when pipette tip is still in the standard solution ⟹ no air at tip of pipette tip
      • put pipette tip at the very bottom of micro-insert, pipet while slowly pulling out

    • 1.5 h for one analysis

      • 10 injections
      • 9 mins/peak

    • password: picarro

    Procedures

    1. Turn on
      1. open app: Start Instrument, CRDS Data Viewer appears
        • Settings–Change GUI Mode from Standard to Service (psw: picarro)
        • choose: H2O, OutletValve, WarmBoxTemp, CavityTemp
      2. connect vaporizer to N2 cylinder via metal pipe line
      3. vacuum pump for vaporizer (A0211)
      4. turn on N2 supply by open the Swagelok toggle valve on the metal pipe line and the cylinder valve, DO NOT change gas regulator
      5. vaporizer: 10s mins to reach 110 °C (external heater powered by 12V)
      6. wait 1–2 h until H2O < 100 ppm
        • tune N2 pressure by outlet valve of gas regulator (ca. 2.5 psi, never >5 psi) ⟹ mean H2O ≈ 20,000 (±500) ppm (decreases as N2 pressure increases)
    2. Autosampler (A0325, open Autosampler before Coordinator)
      1. Replace vaporizer injection port septum (per 200–300 injections, Restek 23878; can last for 1000 injections before H2O suddenly decrease), replace metal salt liner (+ ⌀ ? O-ring), finger-tighten
      2. Chg Syringe (10 µl, Trajan 002980)
        1. take off syringe
          • release middle and top latch
          • lift bottom lever before passing needle through 2 holes
        2. rinse ×3 with solvent, rinse ×3 with RO water
        3. install syringe
        4. Swap done
      3. Method: Picarro Z
    3. Edit sequence in Run List folder
    4. Open Coordinator Launcher
      • ! close and reopen before loading new sample descriptions
      • O17 high precision mode
      • New Output File...
      • Load Sample Descriptions
    5. Autosampler:
      1. make sure method is Picarro Z
      2. Run
    6. Check and tune during the measurement of the first DI water
      • H2O ≈ 20,000 ppm (pump to ~300 ppm)
      • peak shape: …
      • Outlet valve: 27000 when pumping, 24000 when measuring
      • Instrument Status
        • Warm Box T: 45.000 °C
        • ! stable Cavity T: 79.994–80.004 °C
        • Cavity P: 50 Torr
      • data:
        • SD δ18O = 0.1‰, δD = 1‰, δ17O = ?‰
    7. Post run
      1. if idle for long time, reverse “Turn on” step 4–2 (leave vaporizer on)
      2. rinse syringe
      3. sonicate salt liner (remove O-ring first)

    Clean vaporizer

    1. reverse “Turn on” step 5–2
      • wait ?h for cooling down
    2. soak with DI water (add ? HOAc)
    3. solenoid valves (Gems Sensors & Controls A2016-MM3-V-C203), O-rings

    Troubleshooting

    software freezing

    • do not use the Windows Task Manager to kill. Use “Stop Instrument” in the Diagnostics folder (Stop software but keep driver running)
    • restart: shutdown Windows, wait a few seconds, press power button
